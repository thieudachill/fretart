FRETART GitHub ↗

Your fingers are already drawing.

FretArt turns a webcam and a guitar into live screen-print art — every fingertip tracked, every pluck heard. Free and open source. Runs entirely in your browser; nothing leaves your machine.

Note — masthead & hero

  • The plan's original hero ("transparent ink video over app footage") died in Phase 5 — gen models output no alpha. Adaptation: the riso loop IS the masthead print, full-bleed; real app footage gets its own full-bleed band further down instead.
  • prefers-reduced-motion: video never autoplays, hero-still-3 shows instead. Same swap on data-saver / very slow connections (poster is the LCP either way).
  • All JPEGs sit on mix-blend-mode: multiply, so their cream melts into the page paper — no visible plate edges despite JPEG having no alpha.
  • Headline misregistration is code (text-shadow on the --fa-offset direction, amber, up-left — one direction everywhere, per the discipline rules).
  • Display face: the mock uses your system's heaviest grotesque; the build self-hosts one display weight (~30 KB woff2, Archivo Black or similar) to echo the wordmark. Body text stays on the app's system stack — zero extra font weight.

Note — the three moments

  • Each moment pairs a spot illustration (the promise) with a real capture (the proof). The page stays honest: illustration never pretends to be a screenshot.
  • Art sides run left, right, right — the alternation breaks exactly once, at 03 — discipline rule 2: repetition with one variation.
  • 03 uses riso2-splash-1 ("sound spreading through matter") as its spot. If it reads too abstract next to the two hands, fallback: lead 03 with the capture and drop the spot.
  • Suggested captures: Line Drawing still (S), Pluck Bloom clip with audio (R), Print Pyramid still (S) — your call, slots take either.

Build spec (8b, after your go)

  • Stack: Astro static site in landing/ (own package.json; root repo stays the app). Zero client JS except the video lazy-load — the page is genuinely a printed sheet.
  • Deploy: wrangler → Cloudflare Workers static assets; GitHub Action redeploys on landing/** changes. Targets: Lighthouse ≥95 perf/a11y, loads on a phone.
  • Weight budget (target ≤ 2.5 MB, hero loop dominates):
